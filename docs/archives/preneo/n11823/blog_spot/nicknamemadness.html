March 6th, 2023. "Nickname Madness"

I've had alot of nicknames over the years. So lets go over them, ok? ok.

Quick disclaimer that this is probably the most open ill get about anything. if more i'm kind of just throwing myself under the bus.

Any names will also be anonymous for the sake of not throwing them under the bus too.

Okay, So it's 2016. And your older sister makes a persona about like a DJ. Like the little copycat i was, I joined in. We had our initials in the name. So for the sake of this, These initials will be KJ And PG. (KJ being me, and PG being my sister.)

My sister had hers as DJPG, And i joined in with DJKJ. This name stuck with me for awhile.

Big skip coming, Since i never used any real usernames but for my real name.

Late 2020, Early 2021? Something like that. That was when "External" came along. I snatched it from my sister, and it just stuck with me for a long time. I made alot of cool friends, Some left, Some stayed with me.

It's now around Probably early 2022, I made alot more usernames, The ones i can remember are Chaoluris, CHA03_REMA1NS, Luther, ONICORN_MANIA, Nova And Kai. So lets also get into those!!!!

Luther is first, Since thats what i use the most. Luther was kind of supposed to be a different name, But it ended up this way. I was into geometry dash when creating luther. I had played a level with the name "clutterfunk" in it (not that level tho) and i died in the middle of clutterfunk being shown. And i saw "lutter". You can kind of see the name i wanted to go for. Lutter is its own character too, but thats basically just a genderbend of luther.

Ok, now We'll get into Chaoluris. I'm not fully sure how i got this name, but i'm pretty sure my sister gave me a list of names, and i picked one, which was chaoluris. I don't use the name that often, but it may bring a comeback...

CHA03_REMA1NS doesn't have much of a backstory. I was bored, it was late, and i wanted to grab a quick name to use. I wanted something to do with "CHAOS" and something to do with "REMAINS", And with numbers. So i just... Mashed the two together and got CHA03_REMA1NS.

ONICORN_MANIA is a weird one. I was playing a minecraft server with my sister, and i mixed two words together, Now im not fully sure what those two words were exactly, But its fun to look back on.

Nova was one when i was hating my name. I didn't like it. I already had a new name for myself, But i slowly started not liking that one.

Now kai. I don't "mind" kai, but i still dont like it as much. Its still nice though.

God, Sorry for the long one on 2022. But now its 2023. Probably is right now as you're reading this!

Now we have hkrk40k. Its still pretty early in 2023, so i dont have any figured out.

My sister had made a channel for a small group including us. And my name in that was hkrk40k. So naturally i just went with having that.

Its the end now, You can leave!!! Thanks for listening.
